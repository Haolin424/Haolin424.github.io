--- layout: archive title: "Research" permalink: /research/ author_profile: true --- {% include base_path %} The life cycle of sedimentary rocks is closely related to Earth's surface processes and biogeochemical cycling. Over geologic timescales, the formation and breakdown of sedimentary rocks can regulate atmospheric CO2 and O2 levels, influencing planetary habitability. My research integrates microanalytical and bulk-rock approaches to examine how sedimentary rock (particularly organic carbon-bearing mudstone) geochemistry evolves during deposition, burial (and diagenesis), and weathering.
